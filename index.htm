Lore

Unknown Time / Date

Officer Bhat's Report - Sagar Prahari Bal - [Translated]

-
-

Throughout the world, there are many unexplored places that hold mysteries and dangers. One such place, would be the Sentinel Island. The Sentinelese people are a kind of people that prefer to live autonomously. Typically, they stay within the forest and remain close-knit with one another. However, local fisherman in the area reported witnessing strange activity on the island. They claim to have witnessed half of the Sentinelese community fleeing the forest. Recently, I decided to make the trip and investigate this matter myself. The reports appear to be honest...As I have witnessed this unusual activity from the Sentinelese. To my knowledge, it appears that they may be avoiding something within the forest.

Additionally, I have noticed them patrolling the area and watching the forests for any dangers. They appear to be unusually quiet with a strong lack of aggression. As they flee to the beaches, I have noticed that our estimations of their numbers appears to be inaccurate...Or these numbers have significantly dropped.

I have decided to continue my investigation and complete a further report.

Unknown Time / Date

Officer Bhat's Report (CONT.) - Sagar Prahari Bal - [Translated]

Today, I had noticed a strange occurrence on the island. While on my search I witnessed a Sentinelese woman running from the forest. She was covered in blood and appeared to have bitemarks on her forearm. It looked as if the wound was recent and she ran to the shore for help. Upon meeting her people, they immediately executed her. Following this, the Sentinelese people burnt her remains.

This was incredibly unusual as they do not enact any atrocities against one another.

Later in the day, I saw the brushes moving with my binoculars. I could not tell what it was but it appeared to be human...Mostly. Whatever human that was, it had strange spores and fungi imbedded into it's skull. Before I could make sense of what it was the creature returned to the forest. I suspect whatever this creature is has something to do with the strange behavior of the Sentinelese.

Further reports will follow.

Equinox PVP Server Rules

Last Update: 8/21/2024

Player VS Player(PVP) Rules: When TWO or MORE players are engaging IN ANY HOSTILE INTERACTION

▪ PVP requires admin oversight. Failure to adhere to this rule will result in a penalty.
▪ Absolutely NO KOS.
▪ Requires roleplay and IC motivations; cannot be performed due to OOC bleed.
▪ ABSOLUTELY NO COMBAT LOGGING. (Repeated offenses will result in a ban.)
▪ If all requirements are properly met… PvP deaths are permadeath unless the assailant is willing to compromise… If so any agreed upon wounds will be applied to the victim.

Last Update: 8/21/2024

Torture Roleplay: Requires proper OOC coordination and approval

▪ Is allowed with proper OOC coordination and approval from all parties… (I.E., the victim must consent OOCly.)
▪ Admin oversight can be requested; but it is not required.
▪ Sexual assault is not allowed by any means.

Last Update: 8/21/2024

Thievery/Banditry: Banditry is allowed in The Equinox... It should be a high-risk/high-reward scenario.

▪ If you find any items that are unprotected by safehouse or vehicle claims...They are up for grabs.
▪ A victim of theft is allowed to defend their loot. This defense should fall proper PVP rules and request admin oversight. There is to be NO KOS.
▪ The scene is to be put on pause if an admin is not readily available.
▪ You are not permitted to steal lore items. (Be reasonable-- take what you need. Do not take everything a person owns. To violate this rule is a reportable offense.)

Last Update: 8/21/2024

Raid Rules: Requires a raid ticket before any raiding.

▪ Requires a raid ticket before any raiding.
▪ The victims of the raid must have at least half of their party online for a raid ticket to be accepted.
▪ All PVP rules must be followed… PVP Deaths are perma… (With potential exceptions.)
▪ The party being raided has the right to evacuate…The raiders will not be notified.
▪ The raiding party is not allowed to raid without in-character knowledge of the other group’s base location.
▪ Metagaming base locations is not allowed…

Last Update: 8/21/2024

Command/demands: The Equinox follows a set of command and demand rules...

▪ An aggressor is allowed to make a set of commands that the other party must follow...
▪ If you comply with the aggressor's rules-- you cannot be perma-deathed.
▪ There must be OOC coordination.
▪ The aggressor will give three warnings...otherwise PVP will ensue...As per the PVP rules... an admin must be ticketed and present at the scene...
